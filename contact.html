Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

No. We welcome walk-ins only.

Can I order cakes online?

Yes. Order through our Cakes page and collect your order from either café.

Do you have vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes. Availability changes regularly, so feel free to ask our team during your visit.

Can I bring my laptop?

Absolutely. We love seeing people work from our cafés. Just be mindful during busy hours, as seating is limited and many guests come to enjoy breakfast or lunch.
