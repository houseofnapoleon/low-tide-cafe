OUR STORY

We built the café we always wanted to visit.

Low Tide started with a simple idea: create a welcoming neighbourhood café where great coffee, homemade food and genuine hospitality come together.

We work with independent European roasters, bake our pastries and cakes in-house every day, and keep our menu intentionally focused so every dish receives the attention it deserves.

Today you'll find us in Fuengirola and Mijas Pueblo, welcoming locals and travellers looking for a slower start to the day.

Our Values

Three things that shape everything we do.

Specialty Coffee

We work with independent European roasters and rotate our coffees throughout the year, always choosing beans we'd happily brew for ourselves.

Quality Over Quantity

Rather than offering everything, we focus on doing a smaller menu exceptionally well. From brunch to cakes, everything is prepared with care and attention to detail.

Community

Low Tide is a place to slow down, meet friends, work for a while or simply enjoy a quiet coffee. We're proud to be part of our local communities in Fuengirola and Mijas Pueblo.
